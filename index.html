About Maclean Car Parts

Founded in 2020, Maclean Car Parts has become a trusted name in premium engine components and half cuts, serving customers around the world—especially in the UAE.

Based in North Maclean, Queensland, Australia, our expert team sources and inspects high-quality parts from top automotive markets to ensure reliable performance with every order.

Thoroughly Inspected

Every part is carefully checked for quality, performance, and reliability before it reaches our shelves.

Specialist Support

Our knowledgeable team is here to help you find the right fit—backed by real automotive experience.

Reliable International Shipping

We securely pack and ship parts worldwide, with careful handling and trusted delivery to ensure they arrive in top condition.
